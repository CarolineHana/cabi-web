# Gabi & Carō Make Cool Things

Joint portfolio site for **Caroline Hana** and **Gabriela Franco**, MDE candidates at Harvard University (Class of 2027).

## About

This site showcases collaborative projects spanning hardware, wearables, robotics, physical computing, and speculative design — built across the joint Master in Design Engineering program at the Harvard Graduate School of Design and the Harvard John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences.

## Projects

| # | Project | Description |
|---|---------|-------------|
| 01 | HEATFORM | Exploring the intersection of heat and form |
| 02 | GAIA | Natural systems and ecological design |
| 03 | NARA | Handheld e-ink device with custom PCB and scroll wheel |
| 04 | CALM | Designing for stillness and intentional pause |
| 05 | RYTHMBOTS | Robotic music-making exploring rhythm, motion, and sound |
| 06 | SCOOPER | Mechanical design with precision and playfulness |
| 07 | AIRLENS | Wearable optics blending augmented vision and speculative design |
| 08 | LUMINI | Soft light and material texture in an everyday object |
